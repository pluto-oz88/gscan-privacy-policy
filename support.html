Frequently Asked Questions

How many free exports do I get?

You get 8 free exports to try GScan and see how it works with your business cards.

How do I upgrade to unlimited exports?

After using your 8 free exports, tap "Upgrade" on the paywall screen to purchase unlimited exports with a one-time payment.

Can I restore my purchase?

Yes! Tap "Restore Purchase" on the upgrade screen if you've already purchased the app on another device.

What formats can I export to?

GScan can export to iOS Contacts, Microsoft Outlook, Google Contacts, and Apple VCard format.

Is my data private?

Yes! All processing happens on your device. We don't store or send your business card data anywhere.

How do I add my location to a contact?

When editing the Address3 field, tap the location button in the editor to add your current address.

Privacy & Security

GScan processes all business card data locally on your device. No images or contact information are sent to external servers.
